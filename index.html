Discrepancies

We analyzed the discrepancies between the pooled Glassdoor reviews and the Givewell review. For each organization with a Givewell review and a minimum of 10 Glassdoor reviews, we constructed a sentiment compound score and the percentage of sentences with positive and negative sentiments. Then, we got the difference between those three indicators from the pooled Glassdoor reviews and from the Givewell review, for each organization.

The graph below summarizes such results. A positive score indicates that the pooled Glassdoor reviews produce a higher result than the Givewell review.
